MyOrdr Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 1, 2025
Last Updated: July 1, 2025

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information such as your name, email, and login credentials
  • Saved content including favorite orders, tags, and public profile details
  • Usage data such as interactions within the app and activity logs
  • Device information like app version, device type, and IP address

We do not collect payment card details, health data, or private messages as part of MyOrdr.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Operate and improve the MyOrdr experience
  • Personalize your account and saved content
  • Support authentication, account recovery, and security workflows
  • Monitor performance and usage trends
  • Respond to support requests and product feedback

We do not sell your personal information or use it for third-party advertising.

3. How We Share Information

We may share information with service providers who help us operate the app, in anonymized or aggregated form, or when required by law.

4. Data Storage and Security

We use reputable third-party infrastructure providers to store and process data. We retain information only as long as needed for product, security, or legal reasons, and we use reasonable safeguards to protect it.

5. Your Controls

  • You can update or delete saved orders in the app
  • You can delete your account from the app’s settings
  • You can request support or opt-out help by contacting us

6. Third-Party Login and Tools

MyOrdr may support sign-in through providers like Google or Apple. We may receive your basic profile information from those providers to create and maintain your account.

7. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13, and we ask individuals under 16 not to provide personal information. If you believe this has happened, contact us at hellomyordr@gmail.com.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of MyOrdr after changes means you accept the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

Questions about privacy? Email hellomyordr@gmail.com.
